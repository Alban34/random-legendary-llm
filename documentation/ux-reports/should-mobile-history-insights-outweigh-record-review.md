## Title
Mobile History gives too much space to analytics before the user can comfortably review past games.

## Severity
Should change

## Affects
Mobile

## Source
Senior Mobile UX Auditor

## Where it appears
History tab on mobile after at least one setup has been accepted and logged.

## Evidence
In the live mobile review, the History tab showed a compact grouped record card followed by a very long `Insights dashboard` stack containing coverage, overall legendary coverage, and multiple category-by-category ranking cards. The screenshot shows the analytics section consuming most of the phone-length surface after the first record, while the history record list remains comparatively compressed. The tab text also places `Insights dashboard` immediately after the first grouped record rather than keeping record review dominant.

## Why it matters
History’s primary job is to help users review, edit, and understand what they played. On a phone, a long analytics stack quickly turns that tab into a scrolling report, which makes result review and record navigation feel secondary and harder to resume.

## Recommended change
Prioritize the record list on mobile and demote analytics behind a collapsible section, secondary tab, or `Show insights` expander. Keep the first several records easy to scan before the user encounters deeper statistics.

## Expected UX improvement
The History tab becomes easier to use as a practical logbook on phone screens while still preserving access to insights when the user explicitly wants analysis.
