# Legendary: Marvel Randomizer — v1.0.2 Release Notes

**Released: April 17, 2026**

> Fan-made tool. Not affiliated with Marvel or Upper Deck Entertainment.

---

## What is this?

Legendary: Marvel Randomizer is a free, browser-based companion app for players of *Legendary: A Marvel Deck-Building Game*. Open the URL. Get a legal, fresh, collection-aware game setup in seconds. No install. No account. No tracking. Ever.

**[Try it now →](https://alban34.github.io/random-legendary-llm)**

---

## What's in v1.0.2

This release focuses on flexibility and visibility: choose exactly which expansions to randomize from tonight, see where every card in your history comes from, and browse your full owned card pool directly from the Collection tab. The app is also now installable as a standalone app on your phone or desktop.

---

### Install on your device

The app now works as a Progressive Web App. Use your browser's "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App" option on Chrome, Edge, or Safari on iOS to pin it directly to your device. After the first load, the app runs fully offline — no network required at the table.

---

### Active expansion filter — play with any subset of your collection

A new **Play Set** selector on the New Game tab lets you choose which owned expansions to draw from for tonight's session — without touching your permanent collection. Want to run a base-game-only night? Lock in just the Core Set. Next session, add Dark City. Change it again anytime. The filter persists across page reloads. When the selected subset can't produce a legal setup for your player count and play mode, the Generate button is disabled and the app tells you why.

---

### Expansion attribution in history

Every card in a history record now shows a small label with its source expansion. Heroes, masterminds, villain groups, henchman groups, and schemes all get the treatment. No data migration required — labels are derived on the fly from the catalog, so every existing history record shows them immediately.

---

### Card browser in Collection

The Collection tab now has a second view mode: a full card browser listing every individual card from your owned expansions. Switch between two grouping modes:

- **By category** — Heroes, Masterminds, Villain Groups, Henchman Groups, Schemes, each sorted A–Z
- **By expansion** — one section per owned set, cards sorted A–Z within each section

Empty sections are hidden. The mode toggle and active grouping persist for the session.

---

### History outcome filter

A new filter row above the game list on the History tab lets you narrow your records by outcome: **All**, **Won**, **Lost**, or **Pending**. The active count updates instantly. When no records match the active filter, a contextual empty-state message is shown. The filter resets on page reload.
