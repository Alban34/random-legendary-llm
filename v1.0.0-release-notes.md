# Legendary: Marvel Randomizer — v1.0.0 Release Notes

**Released: April 14, 2026**

> Fan-made tool. Not affiliated with Marvel or Upper Deck Entertainment.

---

## What is this?

Legendary: Marvel Randomizer is a free, browser-based companion app for players of *Legendary: A Marvel Deck-Building Game*. Open the URL. Get a legal, fresh, collection-aware game setup in seconds. No install. No account. No tracking. Ever.

**[Try it now →](https://alban34.github.io/random-legendary-llm)**

---

## What's in v1.0.0

This is the first public release. Everything below ships on day one.

---

### Legal setup generation

The generator enforces all official rules automatically — mandatory mastermind leads, scheme constraints, forced villain groups, and every player-count requirement. You will never accidentally deal an illegal setup.

- Full support for 1 to 5 players in standard play
- **Advanced Solo** mode (1 player, 4 heroes, 2 villain groups)
- **Two-Handed Solo** mode (1 player playing a 2-player game, logged as solo)
- Scheme-driven modifiers applied at generation time (forced groups, slot adjustments, rule notes displayed inline)

---

### Collection awareness

Mark the sets you own. The generator only draws from your shelf. Whether you have just the Core Set or every expansion ever printed, the app stays within what you actually have. Collection state persists across sessions and updates live as you add or remove sets.

---

### Freshness-first randomization

Every accepted game is tracked. Heroes, masterminds, villain groups, henchman groups, and schemes you haven't played recently are prioritized. Entities you've never played are always preferred. When the never-played pool runs dry, the generator falls back to the least-played options with an informational notice. Popular cards stop dominating every session automatically.

---

### Forced picks

Lock in a specific hero, villain group, mastermind, or scheme before rolling. The generator fills the remaining slots legally around your pick. Useful for themed nights, personal challenges, or when you simply need to play as Spider-Man.

---

### Play history and result logging

Every accepted setup is logged with full detail — mastermind, scheme, heroes, villain groups, henchman groups, play mode, and player count. After the game, record the outcome: win or loss, score, and free-text notes.

View your history grouped by mastermind, scheme, hero, villain group, or play mode to see patterns in how you play.

---

### Insights dashboard

See your collection at a glance:

- Win rate and average score
- Most-played and least-played entities per category
- Play-mode breakdown
- Total games logged

---

### Data portability

Export a portable JSON backup at any time. Import it on any device. Merge two devices' play histories without losing a single record — duplicates are detected automatically. No cloud required.

---

### Per-category and full reset

Reset usage stats for individual categories (heroes, masterminds, villain groups, henchman groups, schemes) without touching the rest of your data. Full reset clears all state after a confirmation step.

---

### Six languages

The entire UI — not just labels — ships in six languages:

- English
- French (Français)
- German (Deutsch)
- Japanese (日本語)
- Korean (한국어)
- Spanish (Español)

Locale preference is remembered across sessions.

---

### Dark and light theme

Switch between a dark Marvel-aesthetic theme and a light theme from the header. Preference is remembered across sessions.

---

### Keyboard and screen-reader accessible

Full tab-key navigation, ARIA roles, live regions for toast notifications, and visible focus treatment throughout. The app is designed to be usable without a mouse.

---

### Zero friction, zero compromise

- No install
- No login or account
- No subscription
- No ads
- No server — 100% static, runs entirely in your browser
- Your data never leaves your device

## Technical foundation

For developers and contributors:

- **Stack:** Svelte 5 · Vite · static SPA · no server-side code
- **Persistence:** browser `localStorage` under the `legendary_state_v1` key
- **Tests:** Node built-in test runner (27 epic-level test suites) · Playwright browser QC
- **Accessibility:** ARIA roles, live regions, keyboard-navigable throughout
- **Build:** `npm run build` · output is a fully static dist folder ready for any static host or GitHub Pages

Full architecture documentation, data model references, design system, and testing strategy are in the [`/documentation`](./documentation/) folder.
